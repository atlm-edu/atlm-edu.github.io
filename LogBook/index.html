Key Features

Complete Protocol Documentation

Detailed forms for common laboratory techniques with protocol-specific fields and customizable templates.

Custom Protocol Builder

Create your own protocol templates with drag-and-drop interface for any laboratory technique not covered by standard options.

Flexible Export Options

Export your data to PDF, Word, Excel, or print with customizable formatting options for reporting and sharing.

Advanced Search & Filtering

Quickly find experiments with powerful search and filtering capabilities across all fields and metadata.

Reporting & Analytics

Generate insights from your experiments with visual reports and analytics to track progress and patterns.

Collaboration Tools

Share protocols and experiments with team members and collaborate in real-time with permission controls.

Supported Laboratory Techniques

SDS-PAGE

Document protein separation with fields for gel percentage, running conditions, and visualization results.

Western Blotting

Record antibody testing with membrane information, transfer conditions, and detection methods.

PCR

Log DNA amplification experiments with primer sequences, thermocycling conditions, and results analysis.

ELISA

Track immunoassay experiments with antibody details, sample preparation, and quantitative results.

DNA/RNA Extraction

Record nucleic acid extraction protocols with sample sources, extraction methods, and quality assessment.

Custom Protocols

Create your own protocol templates for any laboratory technique not covered by our standard options.
